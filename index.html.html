Carne Molida Carne Molida

Orale, vamos a preparar una Carne Molida bien sabrosa! Oh, como muchos la conocen, "Carne Apache"

Ingredients

  • 1lb Carne molida
  • 8 Limes
  • 3 Roma tomatoes
  • 6 Serranos
  • 1 Red onion
  • Cilantro bunch
  • Optional-1-2 habaneros

Instructions

  1. Set carne molida in a large container & sprinkle a generous amount of salt & pepper.
  2. Cut & squeeze all 8 limes into the container of carne, mixing as you add the lime.
  3. Refrigerate the carne as you clean/rinse & chop the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Starting with the tomatoes, dice them & add them to the container, mixing & refrigerating after o that the lime & tomatoe juice mix well.
  5. Continue the same steps with the onion, serranos & habaneros
  6. Let the Carne Molida marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours so that the carne can cure.
  7. Chop the cilantro & add it once the carne has fully cured.
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  9. Serve with tostadas or Doritos & enjoy.
